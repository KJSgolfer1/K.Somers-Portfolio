About Me

I'm Kyle Somers — a graphic designer and sports management professional serving as Asst. Sports Information Director & Graphic Designer at Grand View University. I create visuals for 28 athletic programs and help tell the story of some of the most dominant programs in NAIA history.

Grand View has won 21 NAIA National Championships — 6 during my time at the university, including 3 in 2025-2026 alone across football, soccer, and wrestling. I designed the complete gameday graphic series for the 2025 football season and created award, event, and milestone graphics across multiple sports programs.

My work lives at the intersection of athletic storytelling, brand identity, and operational excellence — every piece built to generate energy and represent a championship-caliber program.
